IHL MUN Brand Guidelines

1. Introduction

Welcome to the official brand guidelines for the International House Lyceum Model United Nations (IHL MUN). This document outlines the visual identity, core elements, and design standards that define our digital presence.

2. Core Identity

Mission: To provide a platform for young diplomats to develop skills in diplomacy, negotiation, and critical thinking while engaging with global issues.

Tone: Professional, Diplomatic, Modern, Prestigious.

4. Typography

We use a combination of three distinct typefaces to create hierarchy and character.

Headings (Oswald)

IHL MODEL UNITED NATIONS 2026

Usage: Page titles, section headers, impact text. Style: Uppercase often used for emphasis.

Body Text (Manrope)

The conference provides a platform for students to develop their skills in diplomacy.

Usage: Paragraphs, long-form text, UI labels. Characteristics: Clean, modern, highly readable.

5. Logo Usage

  • Primary Logo: The graphical emblem plus the "IHL MUN" wordmark.
  • Dark Mode (Default): White text with Gold emblem elements.
  • Light Mode: Not supported (Brand is Dark-Theme only).

6. UI Components

Buttons

Primary: Gold Background (#FFC72C), Dark Text. Rounded Pill shape.

Secondary: Transparent Background, Muted Border. Hover effects enabled.

Visual Effects

Glows: Subtle use of radial gradients to add depth.

Glassmorphism: Used sparingly on overlays.

7. Accessibility

All text must maintain high contrast against the dark background. Primary actions (Gold) are designed to stand out clearly against Deep Blue.
